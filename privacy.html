Privacy Policy

Let your imaginations run wild! Aspire and Create to Inspire!

Last updated: June 28, 2026

TechnoPlusLabs ("we", "our", or "us") operates this blog site. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit and how we handle it.

1. Information We Collect

Analytics (Google Analytics 4). We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use this site. GA4 collects data such as pages visited, time on site, approximate geographic location (country/city level), device type, and browser. This data is aggregated and used solely to improve the site. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to collect this data.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Theme preference. When you use the dark/light/auto theme toggle, your preference is saved in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or anyone else.

No forms or accounts. This site has no sign-up, login, comment, or contact forms. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any other personal information directly.

2. Cookies

Google Analytics 4 may set cookies (such as _ga and _ga_*) in your browser to distinguish visitors and track sessions. These cookies typically persist for up to 2 years. Our theme toggle uses localStorage, not cookies.

3. Third-Party Services

We load assets and services from the following third parties. Your browser connects to their servers and their privacy policies apply:

  • Google Analytics 4 — visitor analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy and GA4 data practices.
  • Google Fonts — serves the Plus Jakarta Sans typeface. Google may log your IP address when the font is requested. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • jsDelivr CDN — serves Bootstrap CSS/JS and Bootstrap Icons. See jsDelivr's Privacy Policy.
  • LinkedIn — the LinkedIn icon in our footer links to an external LinkedIn profile. Clicking it takes you to LinkedIn's platform, governed by LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves (e.g. if we add analytics or a newsletter). The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us on LinkedIn.
